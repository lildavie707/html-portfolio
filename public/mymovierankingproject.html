The Best Movies According to David

My All-Time Top 3 Movies

Tommy Boy

This is my absolute favorite movie of all time. It is a very funny movie with a lot of memorable lines.

Pretty Woman

Even though this is a chic flic and a romantic comedy. It is one of the best movies ever made. It is funny and it shows that even someone who is from a life that is usually seen as disgusting and immoral can be a good person and if given the opportunity can make a positive impact.

With Honors

This movie shows that there are more important things in life than getting honors for work or school achievements. An unexpected friendship changes the course of a student at Harvard and he gives up his opportunity to graduate with honors from Harvard University. You will CRY so bring your tissues.
